## 2. Core Service Offerings

*   **Chill Zone:** Privacy-fenced garden, clothing optional, non-judgmental atmosphere.
*   **Ice Cold Plunges:** 6’ round pool, seats 8, water changed daily.
*   **Hot/Cold Showers:** Bamboo-fenced private showers with biodegradable tea tree/lavender wash.
*   **Atmosphere:** Fire bowl center, bean bags, thoughtful night lighting (candles/fairy lights).

### [Sauna Etiquette & Terms](https://hellosunshinesauna.com/hello-sunshine-sauna-terms-and-conditions)
*   Shower before entry.
*   Bring two towels (one for sitting, one for drying).
*   No personal water pouring on stones.
*   Full refunds available until the day of the festival.
